FARMSTANDS

    El Dorado County Farmers Market — A farmers market isn't a place, it's a weekly event. Vendors change constantly, the map pin has no fixed location that means anything specific, and Saturday-morning-only doesn't fit how people use this guide.
    Gold Hill Vineyard Farmstand — We already have Gold Hill Vineyard as a winery entry. The farmstand is at essentially the same address. This is a sub-feature of something already on the map. Merge the olive oil detail into the winery note and drop this.
    South Fork Farm — "Available through weekly farmers markets and CSA subscription." This isn't a place you visit. You subscribe to it. There's no address to go to, no tasting room, no hours. It's a great farm, but it's not mappable.
    Argyres Orchard — Open 16 days per year, two weekends only. A spot that's accessible for roughly 48 hours annually shouldn't be a permanent map entry.
