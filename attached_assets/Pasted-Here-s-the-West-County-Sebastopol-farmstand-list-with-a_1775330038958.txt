Here’s the West County / Sebastopol farmstand list with all available addresses:

🍎 GOLD RIDGE ORGANIC FARMS
3387 Canfield Road, Sebastopol · 707-823-3110
goldridgeorganicfarms.com
70+ heirloom apple varieties plus estate olive oil. Farm shop open Fridays & Saturdays, 10am–4pm. Annual Heirloom Apple Festival in September. ￼

🍏 WALKER APPLES
10955 Upp Road, Sebastopol · 707-823-4310
Farmed by the Walker family for nearly 70 years, with 25+ apple varieties. Harvest starts late July with Gravensteins, continuing through fall. ￼

🐐 REDWOOD HILL FARM / CAPRACOPIA
5440 Thomas Road, Sebastopol
redwoodhillfarm.org
Spring is baby-goat petting season, with tours covering regenerative farming and cheesemaking. Small farmstand with olive oil, goat milk soaps, and flowers. ￼

🌱 EARTHseed FARM
3175 Sullivan Road, Sebastopol · 707-829-0617
earthseedfarm.org
Celebrates African agricultural traditions with U-pick days, tours, farm stays, and a seasonal farm store. ￼

🌸 WEST COUNTY COMMUNITY FARM
1720 Cooper Road, Sebastopol
westcountycommunityfarm.com
Grows 20+ varieties of edible and cut flowers, 20+ herb varieties, and produce ranging from strawberries to pumpkins. CSA memberships available. ￼

🍓 LAO SAETERN’S STRAWBERRY STAND
Highway 12 near Sebastopol (roadside stand, no fixed address)
A beloved wood shack brimming with just-picked super-sweet strawberries. Expect lines and occasional one-basket-per-person rationing. ￼

🫐 KOKOPELLI FARM
Sebastopol (check social media for exact location)
A u-pick blackberry farm, great for summer picking. ￼

🍄 MYCOPIA MUSHROOMS
3620 Frei Road, Sebastopol
The first company to commercially cultivate Shiitake mushrooms in the US. Grows maitake, trumpet royale, oyster, and clamshell varieties. ￼

🐔 ALCHEMIST FARM
Sebastopol (pickup at farmstand — check alchemistfarm.com for location)
alchemistfarm.com
Specializes in laying hens producing a variety of colored eggs. Preorder chicks online and pick them up at the farmstand. ￼

🌾 SINGING FROGS FARM
1301 Ferguson Road, Sebastopol
A no-till, ecologically intensive farm growing 80+ varieties of vegetables, 20 varieties of fruit, and 70 varieties of flowers. ￼

🛒 SEBASTOPOL SUNDAY FARMERS MARKET
Sebastopol Plaza, Weeks Way, Downtown Sebastopol
Year-round Sundays, 9am–1:30pm. Eclectic mix of produce, herbal remedies, artisan goods, and global food carts. ￼​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​
